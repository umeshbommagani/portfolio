Project: Classification Of Diabetic Retinopathy Stages Using Hybrid CNN Transformer Ensemble Models

Description

Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) is a leading cause of blindness among working-age adults. In this project, we developed a hybrid model that combines Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Transformers for the classification of diabetic retinopathy stages. Our approach leverages the strength of CNNs in feature extraction and the sequence modeling capability of Transformers.

Approach:

  • Data preprocessing using image augmentation techniques.
  • Utilized a CNN backbone for feature extraction from retinal images.
  • Implemented a Transformer model to capture global dependencies in the image data.
  • Ensemble of CNN and Transformer models to improve accuracy and reduce overfitting.

Results:

  • The model achieved an accuracy of 92% on the test dataset, significantly outperforming traditional methods. The ensemble method reduced the error rate and improved generalization on unseen data.
